About the Registry

Why This Registry Exists

The Toyota Soarer and Lexus SC are among the most iconic Japanese grand tourers ever produced. From the original Z10 in 1981 through to the final SC430 in 2010, these cars represent nearly three decades of Toyota's commitment to luxury performance.

The Soarer Registry is a community-driven project to document every surviving example in the world — building a permanent, searchable record for owners, historians, and enthusiasts alike.

The Chassis Generations

Z10 1981–1985

The original Soarer. Set the template for the grand tourer formula with straight-six engines and a distinctive wedge profile.

  • 2000GT — 1G-EU 2.0L I6
  • 2000GT-Extra — 1G-GEU 2.0L DOHC
  • 2800GT — 5M-GEU 2.8L I6
  • 3000GT — 6M-GEU 3.0L I6

Z20 1986–1991

Twin-turbo technology arrived. First Japanese production car with a digital instrument cluster.

  • 2.0GT — 1G-GEU 2.0L DOHC
  • 2.0GT Twin Turbo — 1G-GTEU
  • 3.0GT — 7M-GEU 3.0L DOHC
  • 3.0GT Twin Turbo — 7M-GTEU

Z30 1991–2000

The most iconic generation. Sold globally as the Lexus SC300/SC400. The primary focus of this registry.

  • SC300 / 3.0GT — 2JZ-GE 3.0L NA
  • SC400 / 4.0GT — 1UZ-FE 4.0L V8
  • Soarer 2.5GT-T — 1JZ-GTE TT
  • Soarer 2.5GT-T Four — AWD

Z40 / SC430 2001–2010

The final chapter. Retractable hardtop design with a 4.3L V8. A luxury grand tourer in a different mold.

  • SC430 — 3UZ-FE 4.3L V8
  • Retractable hardtop only
  • LHD (Lexus) and RHD domestic

How It Works

  1. Create an account — free and quick
  2. Register your car — fill in as much detail as you know
  3. Upload photos — help document the car for history
  4. Update anytime — keep information accurate as things change

VIN and Frame Numbers are unique identifiers in the registry — no two entries can share the same number, ensuring the integrity of the data.

Contributing

If you know of a Soarer or SC that is not yet in the registry, you can register it even if you are not the owner. Use the "current owner name" field to credit the correct person. The registry is a community resource for everyone.
